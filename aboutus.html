About us

Welcome to CO² Footprint! We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to combating climate change by promoting transparency and accountability.
Our mission is to analyze and publish CO₂ data from companies, empowering individuals and decision-makers with the knowledge they need to drive meaningful action.

Our Mission

We believe knowledge is the foundation of change. By gathering, analyzing, and publishing CO₂ data, we aim to shed light on how businesses contribute to climate change.
Our work seeks not only to inform but also to inspire companies and policymakers to take concrete steps toward reducing emissions and embracing sustainability.

What We Do
  • Data Analysis and Publication: We collect and verify CO₂ data from companies, making their environmental impact transparent.
  • Education and Awareness: Through accessible reports and interactive tools, we help the public understand the significance of CO₂ emissions and their role in climate action.
  • Advocating Sustainable Solutions: We collaborate with businesses, policymakers, and communities to accelerate the transition to a carbon-neutral economy.
Why We Do It

Climate change is one of the greatest challenges of our time, and we believe everyone has the right to understand how companies contribute to the crisis.
Transparency is a powerful tool for change, and our goal is to empower society with the information needed to demand accountability and create a sustainable future.

Join Us

We are a movement driven by people like you - individuals who refuse to stand by and watch. Together, we can make a difference.
Join us in the fight against climate change and help create a greener world.
